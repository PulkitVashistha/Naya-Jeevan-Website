Team

Raj Kumar Sharma (Founder)

The Naya Jeevan Rehabilitation center was founded and funded by Mr. Raj Kumar, who has worked diligently in the eshtablishment and rise of this initiative. He himself is a survivor of throat cancer and is under remission right now, but never gave up on his mission to provide this service to people. Despite his inability to speak, he has not let any obstacle come in the way of this rehab center's provision. He is an inspiration to his family members and to others who have witnessed his journey.

Sujata Sharma

Another key stone in the functioning and survival of this initiative has been Mrs. Sujata Sharma w/o Mr. Raj Kumar Sharma. Her contribution in the management of the facilities at the center and her forever welcoming and caring nature have been the face of this rehab center. Providing opportunities for the patients to grow and managing the daily goings of the place, all the while running her household, has been a never ending fight for her. She is the supporting pillar of this movement.
